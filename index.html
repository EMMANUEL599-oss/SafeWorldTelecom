Company overview

Safe World Telecom

Safe World Telecom is a Kenyan-based telecommunications company and an authorized Safaricom Dealer and M-Pesa Agent. Established in 2007, Safe World Telecom has grown into one of the most trusted and leading distributors of Safaricom products and services in Kenya. We offer a comprehensive range of mobile phones, telecommunication accessories, internet solutions, and financial services. Serving both urban and rural areas, our 20 branded outlets and over 200 M-Pesa sub-agencies ensure we meet the diverse needs of customers nationwide with innovative, affordable, and reliable communication solutions.

COMPANY OVERVIEW

  • Mobile Devices: Offering a selection of mobile phones, including Safaricom-branded devices (like the Neon series) and leading global brands such as Samsung, Huawei, Tecno, and Infinix.
  • SIM Cards and Airtime: As an authorized Safaricom dealer, we distribute Safaricom SIM cards, airtime, electronic vouchers (e-vouchers), and scratch cards.
  • M-Pesa Services: With over 200 M-Pesa sub-agents, we offer essential financial services such as deposits, withdrawals, money transfers, bill payments, and mobile banking, ensuring financial inclusion across Kenya.
  • Internet Solutions: Providing high-speed internet connectivity through Safaricom’s Fibre and LTE services for homes and businesses, alongside mobile broadband devices like modems, routers, and MiFi units.
  • Mobile Accessories: From chargers and earphones to power banks and screen protectors, we offer a wide variety of mobile accessories to complement our device sales.
  • Mobile Insurance & Financing: Through programs such as Safaricom’s “Lipa Mdogo Mdogo” and DigiLife mobile insurance, we provide customers with affordable options for accessing and protecting their mobile devices.
  • Business Solutions: Catering to corporate clients by offering enterprise telecommunication solutions, bulk SMS services, and customized USSD solutions to enhance communication efficiency.

We operate 20 branded retail outlets and over 200 M-Pesa sub-agencies across Nairobi, Rift Valley, Western, and Nyanza regions. Our extensive network allows us to deliver Safaricom products and services, ensuring communication accessibility for all, from densely populated urban areas to remote rural communities.

Integrity: We uphold honesty and transparency in all business operations.
